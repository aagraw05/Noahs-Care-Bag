WHAT WE ARE DOING

NOAH'S CARE BAGS AT A GLANCE

To support families going through a crisis by providing necessary items to parents/guardians to care for themselves allowing them to focus on their child who is hospitalized. We provide shampoo, condition, soap, lotion, toothbrush, toothpaste, and some candy along with a positive message to parents/guardians of children admitted to Children's Hospital at Upstate Medical University.

About Noah's Care Bags

Noah started Noah's Care Bags after he noticed the need for personal care items for parents with kids in the hospital battling serious illnesses.
The bags contain the everyday items intended to make it so there's one less thing to worry about.
